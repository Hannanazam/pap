Event Information

This page features events sponsored by PAP University, the PAP Alumni Association (PAP), PAP Clubs, and PAP Shared Interest Groups (SIGs). PAP Clubs and PAP SIGs are alumni-governed and independently financed.

In order to slow the potential transmission of the virus and protect vulnerable populations from exposure, the PAP is currently only hosting virtual events. PAP urges all members of the PAP community to follow CDC and local health department guidance regarding in-person gatherings.
