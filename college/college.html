Extraordinary Graduates

Meet our newest alumni, who persevered, gave back, surprised themselves, made art, and dreamed big through unimaginable years at Harvard.

Harvard is a lifelong journey with countless avenues to explore. Through the Harvard Alumni Association (HAA), there are so many ways for College alumni and students to connect with the Harvard community and grow networks beyond Cambridge. The HAA is the official association for the more than 400,000 Harvard alumni around the world. College alumni and students already have a membership just by attending Harvard.

College alumni are invited to learn more about the ways in which the HAA in here for them. Whether you are looking to build or expand your Harvard community, reconnect with classmates, mentor students, engage in lifelong learning, or stay informed on Harvard news—the HAA is here to keep you connected to all things Harvard.

Current students can explore ways to get involved and connect with Harvard's diverse alumni community. You can also volunteer with the HAA and the Harvard College Fund through the Senior Class Committee. Help support Harvard's legacy of innovative teaching, research, and discovery by volunteering with your class's fundraising committee or by making your annual gift today.
